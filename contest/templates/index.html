Getting started

Before you can start competing, you should have a Dogecoin wallet and some coins in it to pay entrance fee. Don't worry, you will get those just for creating a new wallet on DogeAPI or taking walk through a few Dogecoin faucets.

Good luck!

Fees and prizes

To enter a contest you have to pay an entrance fee. As soon, as the contest ends, the winner becomes a prize, which he can withdraw to the address he provides.

Currently, the service takes no charge for using it and all entrance fees are paid back to the winner. This may change in the future, but you will be warned before on this site.

As using the service is free, we ask for you donation!
