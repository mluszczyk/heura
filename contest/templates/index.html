Getting started

Before you can start competing, you should have a Dogecoin wallet and some coins in it to pay entrance fee. Don't worry, you will get those just for creating a new wallet on DogeAPI or taking walk through a few Dogecoin faucets.

Good luck!

Fees and prizes

To enter a contest you have to pay an entrance fee. We always leave 5D from all entrance fees in every contest. The rest goes on the account of the winner. The 5D stays by us, because we have to pay TXFEE when the winner withdraws his cash. If you know, how to precisely count TXFEE, please, let us know, so we can increase the winner's prize.

This scenario is going to change. You'll be warned before it happen on this site.
