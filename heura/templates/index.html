Getting started

Before you can start competing, you should have a Dogecoin wallet and some coins in it to pay entrance fee. Don't worry, you will get those just for creating a new wallet on DogeAPI or taking a walk through a few Dogecoin faucets.

Good luck!

Fees and prizes

To enter a contest you have to pay a nominal entrance fee (in Dogecoins). As soon as the contest ends, the winner receives a prize, which he can withdraw to the address he provides.

Currently, using the service is free of charge and all participants' entrance fees are paid back to the winner. This may change in the future, but you will be warned before it happens on this site.

As using the service is free, we gently ask for generous donations!
